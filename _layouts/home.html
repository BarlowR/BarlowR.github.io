Hello!

I'm Rob, an adventurer with a passion for exploring and making things . This website is a space for me to put down some thoughts, ideas, and experiences.

What you'll find here:

Projects: When I finish a project, I'll document a bit about the process and the outcome here.

Trip Reports: Here I'll be sharing my experiences from my misadventures around California and wherever else I end up.

Thoughts and Notes: I greatly admire the writing of essayists like Maria Popova, Hanif Abdurraqib, and many many more. While I can't come close to the writing of those I admire, I do like to occasionally write bits and pieces of things down. These posts will be a mix of short reflections and longer thoughts.
